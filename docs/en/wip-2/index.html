KOSPI Fear/Greed Sentiment

A sentiment check based on how far retail net-buying deviates from its usual relationship with KOSPI returns.

What to read

  • Retail investors usually buy more when prices fall and sell more when prices rise.
  • Large deviations from that pattern may mark fear or greed zones.
  • This is not a buy/sell signal. It is a context indicator.

KOSPI sentiment cards

Fear means retail buying is unusually weak during a weak market. Greed means retail buying is unusually strong during a rising market.

Data and method

The model combines KOSPI closing prices and retail investor net-buying amount, then compares the latest value with the usual return-flow pattern.
