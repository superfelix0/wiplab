SK Hynix ADR vs Common Share

Compares the ADR-implied KRW price with the KOSPI-listed common-share price.

What to read

  • ADR and local shares can trade at different prices because of access, timing, FX, and market demand.
  • ADR 1 share represents 0.1 common share, so the ADR KRW value is multiplied by 10 for common-share comparison.
  • The spread is a reference indicator, not a direct arbitrage signal.
