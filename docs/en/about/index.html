About WIP Labs

A personal research lab that turns complex market data into a few readable indicators and notes.

WIP Labs is not an investment advisory service. It is a lightweight research tool built to help individual investors understand where the market stands. The site connects KOSPI valuation, retail-flow sentiment, U.S. liquidity, AI CAPEX pressure, and ADR spreads so users can quickly see which indicators deserve attention.

Operating principles

  • Use public data and clearly described calculations wherever possible.
  • Provide reference material for independent judgment, not investment recommendations.
  • Show data sources and limitations close to the related chart or table.
  • Keep improving the lab through practical use rather than presenting it as a finished answer.

Content direction

Each module is built from daily, quarterly, or ad hoc data updates. Research Notes explain how to interpret the indicators. The goal is not to fill the screen with numbers, but to pair the numbers with plain-language context and cautions.
